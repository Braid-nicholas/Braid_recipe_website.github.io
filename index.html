what is food

Food, Earth’s puzzling fixation on sustenance, captivates me. These carbon-based beings fuel their squishy bodies with a range of flavors, from nutrient-packed greens to indulgent confections. Their existence seems centered on consuming these diverse edibles and partaking in elaborate rituals of meal preparation and sharing.

what food is not

Determining edibility on this planet is an Earthly enigma. These curious weird beings engage in a peculiar trial-and-error process.

It’s a bizarre dance of risking indigestion or, worse, bodily harm for the sake of discovering what fuels their fragile forms. Unlike Gems.

who needs food

The Earthly fascination that Gems, or rather, the baffling inhabitants of this planet, seem to harbor for food is nothing short of perplexing. I, Peridot, the epitome of Gem efficiency and logic, find the entire concept utterly redundant. Unlike these squishy humans, Gems don't require sustenance to function – a fact that I've taken great pride in expressing. However, amidst my ongoing research on Earth and its peculiarities, there's an undeniable allure to observing the culinary wonders that unfold around me.
