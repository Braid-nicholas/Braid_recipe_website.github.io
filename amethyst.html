everything pizza

Ah yes the most adventerous one of all the crystal gems, Amethyst. She is known for her outlandish taste in food and ability to eat just about anything so this pizza is obvioulsy going to be her favorite. How could it not? It has everything...

ingridients

  • 1 store bought pizza dough
  • 1 fully cooked whole salmon
  • 1/4 cup of basil
  • 1/2 cup of marinara
  • 3 tins of sardines
  • 3 tins of sardines
  • 1/4 cup of mushrooms
  • 2 cloves of garlic

instructions

  • Step 1

    Preheat oven to 400F.

  • Step 2

    Roll out fresh dough.

  • Step3

    Spread all of the marinara all over the dough leave a little room for the crust.

  • Step 4

    Do a solid layer of cheese.

  • Step 5

    Add a small amount of all the other toppings.

  • Step 6

    Repeat steps 4&5 until you run out of toppings.

  • Step 7

    Wedge your salmon on either end of your pizza mound to give the appearance of the fish jumping through your pizza.

  • Step 8

    Put your pizza in the oven for forty minutes and wait!

  • Step 9

    After the time is up take your pizza out of the oven and eat it whole.
