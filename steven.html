cookie cat

Cookie Cat isn’t just a snack; it’s my gem-powered comfort food! It’s not just about the ice cream; it’s a link to my human side, grounding me in the midst of the chaos in my life.

ingridients

  • 2 ½ cups all purpose flour
  • ½ cup dark cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon almond extract
  • 4 quarts vanilla ice cream
  • 4 quarts strawberry ice cream

instructions

  • Step 1

    Preheat oven to 325ºF.

  • Step 2

    In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt.

  • Step3

    In a large bowl, beat butter until smooth. Add sugar and mix until light and fluffy.

  • Step 4

    Add eggs one at a time and then mix in vanilla and almond extracts. Mix in flour on a low speed.
  • < h4>Step 5 Shape dough into 2 discs about an inch thick and wrap them in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 1 to 24 hours.

  • Step 6

    Roll out dough to ¼ an inch thick. Cut out cat heads and place them on a lined cookie sheet about an inch apart.

  • Step 7

    Use a small oval cutter to cut eyes out of half of the cookies. Bake for 16 minutes.

  • Step 8

    Remove the cookies from the oven. Cool for 2 minutes the cookie sheet and then transfer them to a wire rack to cool.
