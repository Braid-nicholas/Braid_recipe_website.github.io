pizza bagel

Garnet loves to indulge on the fusion between breakfast and dinner! This is the perfect food for someone who loves to combine foods and try new things!

ingridients

  • 2 Bagels
  • 2 tbs Olive oil
  • 2 tps Garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup Cream cheese
  • 1 cup Pizza sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped Basil
  • 2 tsp Chili flakes *optional*

instructions

  • Step 1

  • Mix your garlic powder and a little bit of olive oil in a shallow dish, and brush the mixture onto both halves of a bagel. Then toast the bagel as desired.

  • Step 2

  • Spread cream cheese onto the toasted bagel, including across the central hole.

  • Step 3

  • Spoon on some tomato pizza sauce, shave some slivers of cream cheese on top, and sprinkle with fresh basil and sprinkle with chili flakes.

  • Step 4

  • Put it back in the oven for around 2 minutes take out and enjoy!
