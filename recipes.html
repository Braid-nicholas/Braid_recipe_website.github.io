recipes

Ugh, so there’s this clod of a friend I have, and their taste in Earth snacks is absolutely primitive. But, surprisingly, they have this weird fascination with organic sustenance, especially these archaic recipes they call “cookies” and “pies.” It’s absurd, really. However, despite my initial disdain for such primitive activities, I’ve found it strangely amusing to concoct these Earthly concoctions for them. The joy on their face when they indulge in my meticulously crafted dishes is quite satisfying, even if it does go against my better Gem judgment. It’s like turning their taste buds into my own personal playground.
