dognut

Before Greg got his big break he told me he used to make a special fusion of foods. He did warn me consuming too many of these may result in an upset stomach.

ingridients

  • 3 store bought donuts
  • 1 hot dog
  • Ketchup & Mustard *optional*

instructions

  • Step 1

    Place your donuts onto a plate.

  • Step 2

    Cook your hot dog if not already cooked

  • Step3

    Gently stand the donuts on their sides

  • Step 4

    Place the cooked hotdog through the three donut holes
  • <

    Step 5

    Drizzle ketchup or mustard.
